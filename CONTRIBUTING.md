branching strategy, and the TDD workflow — live in:

👉 **[`.github/CONTRIBUTING.md`](.github/CONTRIBUTING.md)**

homepage. This root file exists as a conventional entry point for contributors
who look for `CONTRIBUTING.md` at the project root.
